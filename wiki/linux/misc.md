## Fake fullscreen

In Firefox ```about:config```
set ``full-screen-api.ignore-widgets`` to ``true``

## Zen Browser
In ``about:config`` 
### hide top bar window controls

``zen.view.expirimental-no-windows-controls = true``

### show suggestions in search bar

``browser.search.suggest.enabled =  true``

### Increase number of entries in search bar

``browser.urlbar.maxRichResults``

### Ignore the new stupid share window feature

``zen.window-sync.enabled = false``
